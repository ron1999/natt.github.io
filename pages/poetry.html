Natural Jaxtaposition

Small perched on large
Soft resting on rough
Gentle blue against harsh grey
Harmless upon harmful
Weapon and prey
Gift of the skies and guardian of the land
Alligned in perfect position
Such a wonderful composition
Natural Juxtaposition

My Little Light

My little light oh how you shine for me
Come, come! Approach and see the way it glows for me
My oh my little light see how your brightness grows for me
Woe is me little light, you blind me!
Oh Holy blindness! You illuminate me!

My little light oh how you watch out for me
Come, come! Approach and see the way it penetrates the darkness for me
My oh my little light see how you increase in sharpness for me
Woe is me little light, you pierce me!
Oh Holy pain! You live in me!

By Mweya Kamchedzera
